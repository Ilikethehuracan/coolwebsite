About Roblox






Roblox is a free to play video game in which ANYBODY can make their own games! Bring your game out for testing (when it's done), fix all the bugs, then release it so other players can enjoy your creation! Play with your friends and other people! Chat with them (in-game feature) and say anything (moderation included)! Change your avatar and play millions of different games! Best of all, it's available on ALL devices! That includes:
-iPhone
-Android
-iPad
-Tablet
-PC
-Desktop
-Xbox

So, what are you waiting for? Join the millions of people who already like this game!

Developers

David Bazucki is the developer of Roblox. As of 2021, he is still alive at 58 years of age. He has also hired about 500 team members to help him handle Roblox. Sadly, the co-founder of Roblox (Erik Cassel) died in 2013.
